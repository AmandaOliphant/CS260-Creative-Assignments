What is MyRecs?

We've all been there. You're with a group of friends and the conversation turns to an old movie, an up-and-coming TV show, or the latest release from a popular author. And as your friends discuss theories and share excitement, you're left thinking, "Huh?". MyRecs is for those moments, to help you keep track of the things you're missing out on.

How Do I Start?

When somebody recommends something to you, just look it up here and add it to your list. Got an empty list? Browse your friends' Rec Lists to find something new. Don't forget to keep track of the things you love so you can recommend them to others!
