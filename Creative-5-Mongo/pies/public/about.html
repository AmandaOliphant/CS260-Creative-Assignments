Pies at Thanksgiving

Pie-making is a huge part of Thanksgiving at my home; it's been that way for as long as I can remember. It has become more than simply making dessert to top off a feast--it is a time to gather and laugh, and to pass skills from one generation to the next. We always use my great-grandmother's pie crust recipe and fill the shells with many different flavors. On a typical Thanksgiving, we end up making as many pies as there are people at the feast! Everyone has to have their favorite pie at the table, after all. This website is a collection of pie recipes, put together just in time for Thanksgiving. Choose a recipe or add one of your own, and let's get baking at this wonderful time of year!
